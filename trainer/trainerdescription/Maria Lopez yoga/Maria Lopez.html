Maria Lopez - Yoga & Pilates Instructor.

Maria Lopez is a highly experienced Yoga and Pilates instructor with over 8 years of expertise in guiding individuals toward balance, flexibility, and overall wellness. Her classes combine traditional yoga techniques with modern Pilates practices, helping members improve posture, increase core strength, and reduce stress.

Maria believes that fitness is not just about physical transformation but also about creating a mind-body connection. Her sessions are known for stheir calm atmosphere, personalized guidance, and focus on breathing techniques that enhance both physical and mental health.

When she’s not teaching, Maria enjoys exploring nature, practicing mindfulness, and sharing healthy lifestyle tips with the FitZone community.
